My History

I was never interested in computer technologies and programming until when I discovered there's alot that we can do about it when it comes to developing applications and websites. After when I explore the world of coding and experience it myself, I feel like there's alot to be done. From then, coding is part of my life and I've always wanted to push the limits and know more about web and application development.

Hi! My name is Bryan Adam Loh. You can call me Bryan for short, everyone calls me that. Just graduated my Diploma from APU, currently working as an intern in Inverze Business Solution Company. Why I Choose To Be A Programmer?


It's fun!

Yes! Coding can be pretty difficult for beginners and over time we will get bored of it. But the satisfaction of getting the code correctly without any problems is the most exciting part every programmer wishes to experience it. It does help in learning along the way and you'll get what you want in the end.

It's challenging!

Coding can be a completely new thing to most of the beginners because of the unfamiliarity. Along the way there will be difficulties trying to understand how the code works. But with the help of Internet and friends around you, coding can be easier and fun to learn. In the end, what is coding without a little bit of challenges?

It's all about creativity!

Basically you can build or develop anything with programming. All you need is creativity! With the skills and knowledge you've learned, you can build anything, like literally anything. From building a website to developing an mobile application, coding act as a platform to make ur creativity becomes reality.

It's so damn cool!

Trust me. Coding is not all just about sitting infront of your computer and do your thing all day. I've been working in an internship for 3 months, the environment is peaceful. Although it's not as GOOD as Google HQ (who wouldn't want to work in Google?), but it's got some good facilities and the people are friendly. A bonus? It has PS4 which is pretty cool for me. Working in an IT company is pretty dope overall, and it's another learning platform for beginners who want to experience how it's like to be to work in such environment.
